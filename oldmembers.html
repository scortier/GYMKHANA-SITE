MEMBERS OF GUB(2018-2019)

ANIRBAN ROY

THE VICE-PRESIDENT AND HIS FUNCTIONS

  • The Vice-President is a bona-fide final year student elected for a period of one year.
  • He will assist the President and the Chairman(Cultural activities/Sports) in all the Gymkhana matters.
  • He will carry out any functions delegated to him by the President or the higher authority.
  • He shall organize and monitor all student related activities.
  • He is the signatory body forwarding any financial matter to the President.
  • He is the Chief Representative(spokesperson) of the Institute’s student community.
  • He will be the convener of the annual Technical festival Committee of NIT, Silchar along with the General Secretary(Gymkhana).
  • He is the ex-offico member and lead of any student related activity or committee initiated or constructed by the Institute authorities.

ANKAN KISHORE PATHAK

GENERAL SECRETARY AND HIS FUNCTIONS

  • General Secretary is a bona-fide student elected for a period of one year.
  • He is the secretary of the GC. He will convene meetings, prepare agenda and maintain the minutes of the meeting
  • He will co-ordinate the overall functioning and activities of the Gymkhana under the supervision of the Vice-President and the guidance of the President.
  • He will be the convener of the annual Technical festival Committee of NIT, Silchar along with the Vice-President.
  • He will present the annual report at the general body meeting of the Gymkhana..
  • He shall take necessary steps on the above with the consent of the Vice-President.
